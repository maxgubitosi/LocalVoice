How it works

Three keys. Three seconds.

01
Hold Right ⌘
Press and hold the right Command key. The recording capsule appears in the corner of your screen.
⌘ held
02
Speak
Whisper transcribes locally on the Apple Neural Engine. Optionally Qwen3.5 (MLX) cleans it up — also locally.
03
Release
Text is inserted directly into the focused field via AXUIElement. No clipboard, no network.
text → focused field
